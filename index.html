AQW X-Men Guild

We are the AQW X-Men. We are a group of exceptional people; mutants, with exceptional talents. We use our talents to fight for the freedom and protection of the innocent, mutant and human alike. For those who wish to end that freedom, we send this message to you; we may not know who you are, we may not know what you want, but we will find you, and we will end you - we are here to stay and we are always watching!
